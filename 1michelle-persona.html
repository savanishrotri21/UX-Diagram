Michelle

Finance Manager

Age 37 Woman (She/Her) Finance Manager Finance & Professional Services
Persona Two Day 0

Background

Michelle is a finance manager managing accountants and bookkeepers. She is a busy, career-oriented individual. She enjoys growing her career by attending trainings and workshops, enrolling in professional development courses and mentoring peers.

Personality Traits

Career-Oriented High Achiever Mentor Detail-Oriented
"I have very bad migraines which forces me to take time off from work. I've rushed to the hospital because nothing is working. It was my only way out because I didn't know what else to do."

Pain Points

Unexpected migraine attacks that disrupt her work schedule and derail her productivity.
Missing work milestones and goals due to migraine-related absences.
Not being able to commit to tasks because the next impending attack is always at the back of her mind.
Lack of reliable insight into her triggers and symptoms makes it difficult to plan ahead or prevent episodes.
Difficulty communicating the severity and pattern of her attacks to doctors without structured data.
Feels anxious about her professional reputation suffering due to unpredictable health-related absences.

Needs

A simple way to track and monitor her migraine symptoms and triggers over time.
Alerts and insights that help her anticipate and prepare for possible attacks.
A clear record of her attack details to effectively communicate with her doctors.
Educational resources to understand her triggers and reduce episode frequency.
A dashboard summarising her migraine history at a glance to minimise cognitive load.

Goals

Limit the number of migraine episodes she experiences each month.
Reduce the impact migraines have on her professional performance and career growth.
Effectively track, communicate, and manage her health data with medical professionals.
Educate herself on triggers and symptoms to proactively prevent future episodes.
